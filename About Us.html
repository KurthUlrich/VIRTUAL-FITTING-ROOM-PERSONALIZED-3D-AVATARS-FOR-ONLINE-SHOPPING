About Us

Learn more about our mission, vision, and the team behind Virtual Fitting Room.

Our Mission

At Virtual Fitting Room, our mission is to revolutionize the online shopping experience by allowing shoppers to create a 3D model of their body using their own measurements and virtually try on clothes to see how they fit. We aim to reduce the hassle of returns and increase customer satisfaction by providing a more accurate and personalized shopping experience.

Our Vision

We envision a future where online shopping is as seamless and enjoyable as shopping in a physical store. By leveraging advanced technology, we strive to create a platform where customers can confidently purchase clothes that fit perfectly, without the need for guesswork or multiple returns.

Our Story

Virtual Fitting Room was founded in 2024 with the goal of solving one of the biggest challenges in online shopping: finding clothes that fit. Our founders, Gian Harina and Kenneth Ariola, both experienced the frustration of ordering clothes online only to find that they didn't fit properly. They decided to combine their expertise in technology and fashion to create a solution that would make online shopping more enjoyable and accurate for everyone.
